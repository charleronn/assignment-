Pescatarian Dishes

Port of France

Lobster chuncks cooked in a creamy purée blend of caramalized vegetables simmered in tomato, bay leaf, thyme, seafood stock, and white wine. Seasoned with salt and pepper. Topped with freshly minced chives as garnish. Served alone.

Mediterranean Seaside

Fresh mussels (brushed and bearded) cooked in sautéed onions and garlic. Simmered in diced tomatoes and white wine. Seasoned with salt and pepper. Topped with fresh parsley as garnish. Served with grilled white bread on the side.

Greek Summer

Baked salmon topped with marinated lemon feta cheese, diced tomatoes, Kalamata olives, Persian cucumbers, and chopped red onions. Lightly seasoned with salt and pepper. Topped with fresh minced dill. Served on a bed of arugula or spinach.
